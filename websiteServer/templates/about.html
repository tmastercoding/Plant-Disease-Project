About Us

Greetings

We are a team of four students from the Hong Kong Academy for Gifted Education, enrolled in a course offered by the Department of Electrical Engineering at City University of Hong Kong.

HKAGE • CityU EE

What motivated us to start this project

Hunger remains a global challenge. Our research shows that plant diseases can reduce the world’s food supply by up to 40%. We hope to contribute by building tools that help diagnose plant diseases and reduce preventable losses.

Our project

We built a system that lets users upload photos of plant leaves. The system analyzes the image to detect potential diseases and then provides practical guidance on how to address them.

Models and data

  • Object detection and disease classification using YOLO, EfficientNet, and ResNet.
  • Trained and evaluated with open-source datasets, including PlantVillage and PlantDoc from Kaggle.
